SECTION 3 — Usability Expectations for ENGAGIUM
(5-Point Likert Scale)
3.1. Dashboard & Interface Expectations (5 items)
1.	I prefer a simple and clean dashboard layout.
2.	I want the dashboard to show participation summaries at a glance.
3.	I prefer having graphs and charts to visualize engagement.
4.	I want the system to allow filtering by date, class, or participation type.
5.	I want real-time indicators showing which participation types are active.
3.2. Report Preferences (5 items)
6.	I prefer downloadable participation reports (e.g., PDF, CSV).
7.	I want reports to contain totals, frequencies, and participation types.
8.	I prefer reports that highlight the top participants.
9.	I prefer reports that identify students who rarely participate.
10.	I want participation reports to show session-by-session engagement comparisons.
3.3. Tracking Options (5 items)
11.	I want the ability to enable or disable specific participation types.
12.	I prefer having customizable weighting for speaking, chat, and reactions.
13.	I want the option to manually adjust participation scores if needed.
14.	I expect the system to notify me when tracking starts or stops.
15.	I want the system to allow correction or deletion of incorrect participation logs.
